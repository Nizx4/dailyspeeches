The Lost Balloon

Today, I watched my red balloon float away into the sky. At first, I felt sad. But then I realized, sometimes we have to let go to discover new adventures. Like that balloon, our dreams can soar higher than we ever imagined—if we have the courage to release them. Let’s celebrate every new beginning, and trust that wonderful things are waiting just beyond the clouds.
