The Courage to Say Sorry

It takes real courage to admit when we’re wrong and say, “I’m sorry.” Apologies build trust and show strength, not weakness. When we own our mistakes, we open the door to forgiveness and understanding. Let’s practice saying sorry with honesty and kindness—because every brave apology makes the world a little better.

Tip: Make eye contact as you deliver your apology.
