The Art of Listening

Listening is just as important as speaking. When we truly listen, we show respect and build understanding. Let’s practice being present, making eye contact, and letting others finish their thoughts. Good listeners make great friends and leaders. Today, let’s listen with open minds and open hearts.

Tip: Pause and nod to show you’re listening during conversations.
