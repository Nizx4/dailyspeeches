The Power of Listening

We often think that speaking well is the key to good communication, but listening is just as important. When we truly listen, we understand, connect, and build trust. Let’s practice listening with open hearts and minds, so that our words—and our silences—bring people together. Thank you for listening, and for letting others be heard.
