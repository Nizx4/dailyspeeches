The Power of a Compliment

One kind word can change someone’s entire day. Compliments are small gifts we can give for free—so why not share them often? Tell a friend they did a great job, thank your teacher, or smile at a stranger. When we lift each other up, we all rise higher. Let’s make kindness our habit, and watch the world become a brighter place!

Tip: Smile as you deliver your compliment for extra warmth.
