The Inventor’s First Failure

When my first invention didn’t work, I felt disappointed. But then I realized: every mistake is a step closer to success. I learned, I tried again, and I never stopped believing in my ideas. Remember, failure is not the end—it’s the beginning of something new. Keep inventing, keep dreaming, and let your imagination lead you to places you never thought possible!
