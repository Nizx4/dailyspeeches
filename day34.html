The Power of Sharing

Sharing isn’t just about giving things away—it’s about building trust and friendship. When we share our time, our stories, or even our snacks, we show others that we care. Let’s be generous with our kindness and make sharing a part of every day. The more we give, the more we grow together!

Tip: Smile and make eye contact to encourage connection.
