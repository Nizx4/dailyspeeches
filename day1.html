Speech to the Martian Senate – Year 2325

Honorable senators of Mars, today we stand at the dawn of a new era. Our red planet, once a distant dream, now thrives with life and hope. Let us unite our colonies, nurture our children, and reach for the stars together. May our courage inspire generations to come, and may Mars forever be a beacon of peace and discovery. Thank you for trusting in this vision. Together, we will shape the future of humanity—on this world, and beyond.
