The School Election Speech

Fellow students, I stand before you with new ideas and a promise to listen. If elected, I’ll work hard to make our school a place where everyone feels welcome and heard. Let’s create more clubs, celebrate our differences, and help each other succeed. Vote for me, and let’s make this year our best one yet—together!

Tip: Speak with energy and look around the room as you talk.
