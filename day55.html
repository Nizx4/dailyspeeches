The Power of Saying Thank You

Gratitude is a simple way to make the world a better place. When we say “thank you,” we show respect and appreciation. It brightens someone’s day and strengthens our connections. Let’s practice gratitude every day—at home, at school, and everywhere we go. A little thanks goes a long way!

Tip: Make eye contact and smile when you say thank you.
