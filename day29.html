The Superhero’s Secret

Everyone thinks superheroes are fearless, but even heroes get scared sometimes. My secret? I face my fears by asking for help and believing in my friends. True strength is found in kindness, honesty, and working together. So, if you ever feel afraid, remember: you’re never alone, and you’re braver than you think!
